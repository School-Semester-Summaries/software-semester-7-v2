Evidence title: GP - Group Interactions

Evidence description:

Everday at 09:30 we do a stand-up. In this stand-up everyone gets tasks assigned that he works on the entire day. If a task is done you can assign yourself a new task. This gives the rest of the group the idea what others are working on. At the end of the day we do a stand-down where everyone showscases/explains what he has done. This activity tells the group if someone is actively working on the group project or not, depending on how much he has to show.
